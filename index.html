Welcome

Soyeon is a fourth year graduate student in the Social Psychology area. Born and raised in South Korea, she received her B.A. in Psychology and English Language & Literature and also her M.A. in Social Psychology from Yonsei University. She is mainly interested in how ecological threats shape people’s time perspective and behavior, and how the environment creates individual differences in the way people define their sense of well-being. She spends her free time taking yoga lessons, traveling, and collecting funny cat memes.
